Problem

Patient inquiries get scattered before they become patients.

Calls, website forms, referrals, insurance questions, and Google inquiries can land in different places. When follow-up is inconsistent, good opportunities go cold.

Missed follow-ups

Overdue calls and messages can hide inside notes, spreadsheets, or memory.

No quick owner view

Practice owners often have to ask the front desk what happened this week.

Unclear pipeline value

Potential treatment value is hard to see when inquiries are not tracked consistently.

Solution

A focused operating screen for inquiry follow-up.

The Chiropractic Business OS gives the owner and front desk one place to review patient inquiries, see who needs follow-up, and understand weekly practice activity.

  • Track inquiry status and requested service.
  • Schedule the next follow-up date.
  • See overdue and due-today follow-ups.
  • Review estimated treatment value and weekly summary.

FAQ

Common questions from chiropractors.

Does this replace my EHR?

No. The EHR remains the clinical source of truth. This focuses on inquiry and follow-up visibility.

Does it schedule appointments?

No. It helps your team see who needs follow-up so your existing scheduling process can happen faster.

Is this a HIPAA production system?

The demo uses fake data. Production use with real patient data requires separate hosting, privacy, access-control, and compliance planning.

Why not just use a spreadsheet?

Spreadsheets can work. This adds a clearer dashboard, weekly summary, follow-up urgency, and patient-friendly exports.
