# ROI Calculator

Use this lightweight calculator during discovery calls, pricing conversations, or any conversation where a chiropractor asks about missed follow-ups and return on setup cost.

The purpose is to estimate the potential value of improving follow-up consistency. It is a simple sales conversation aid, not a financial promise.

## Input Definitions

- Monthly patient inquiries: the number of new patient inquiries the practice receives in a typical month.
- Average new patient value: the estimated value of one new patient opportunity.
- Missed follow-up rate: the estimated percentage of inquiries that do not receive timely follow-up.
- Recovery rate after using Business OS: the estimated percentage of missed follow-ups that could be recovered by reviewing the follow-up queue consistently.

## Formula

```text
missed inquiries = monthly inquiries x missed follow-up rate
recovered inquiries = missed inquiries x recovery rate
potential recovered revenue = recovered inquiries x average new patient value
```

## Conservative Example

```text
20 inquiries/month
$500 average new patient value
20% missed follow-up rate
50% recovery rate
```

Calculation:

```text
missed inquiries = 20 x 0.20 = 4
recovered inquiries = 4 x 0.50 = 2
potential recovered revenue = 2 x $500 = $1,000/month
```

## Disclaimer

This estimate is intended for discussion purposes only. Results will vary by practice and are not guaranteed.

Actual results depend on inquiry volume, staff usage, follow-up quality, patient demand, and the practice's existing workflow.

Use conservative numbers. The goal is to help the practice owner understand the possible cost of missed follow-ups, not to promise revenue.
