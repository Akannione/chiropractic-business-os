# Discovery Call Simulation Library

Use these 20 simulated chiropractic prospect profiles to rehearse discovery calls before outreach begins.

## 1. Solo Family Chiropractor

- Practice type: family chiropractic.
- Staff size: 1 provider, 1 front desk coordinator.
- Inquiry volume: 15-20 per month.
- Current tracking method: paper notebook and missed-call list.
- Technology openness: medium.
- Primary pain point: follow-ups depend on one staff member remembering.
- Likely objections: "We are too small for software." "My staff may not use it."

## 2. Sports Injury Practice

- Practice type: sports injury and rehab-focused chiropractic office.
- Staff size: 2 providers, 2 staff.
- Inquiry volume: 25-35 per month.
- Current tracking method: Google Sheet.
- Technology openness: high.
- Primary pain point: inquiries come from website, Google, and referrals but are not reviewed weekly.
- Likely objections: "Why not just improve our spreadsheet?" "How do I know this will pay for itself?"

## 3. Insurance-Heavy Practice

- Likely objections: "Does this connect to insurance?" "Is this HIPAA compliant?"
