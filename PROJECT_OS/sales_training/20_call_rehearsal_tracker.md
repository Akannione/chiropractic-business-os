## Completion Rule

Before live outreach, complete at least:

- 5 rehearsals minimum.
- 10 rehearsals preferred.
- 20 rehearsals ideal.

After each rehearsal:

1. Score the call.
2. Write the weakest moment.
3. Record the objection that caused hesitation.
4. Rehearse one better answer before the next call.
