# Qualification Scorecard

Use this quick scorecard during outreach to decide which chiropractic prospects deserve the most follow-up effort.

## 1. Ideal Customer Profile

- Solo chiropractor or small chiropractic practice.
- Uses spreadsheets, notes, inboxes, or memory to track inquiries.
- Receives enough monthly inquiries that missed follow-up matters.
- Wants better follow-up consistency and weekly owner visibility.
- Needs a simple inquiry dashboard, not an EHR, scheduler, billing system, or insurance tool.

## 2. Qualification Criteria

Score each prospect using the detailed scorecard in `PROJECT_OS/prospect_management/qualification_scorecard.md`.

- Practice Size: 15 points
- Monthly Inquiry Volume: 20 points
- Current Tracking Method: 20 points
- Follow-Up Process: 20 points
- Reporting Maturity: 10 points
- Openness to Technology: 15 points

## 3. Scoring System

- 80-100: Ideal Prospect
- 65-79: Strong Prospect
- 50-64: Possible Fit
- Below 50: Low Priority

Prioritize Ideal and Strong prospects for calls, demos, and proposal follow-up. Use Possible Fit prospects for lower-intensity follow-up. Do not spend heavy time on Low Priority prospects unless new information changes the score.

## 4. Red Flags

- Wants an EHR replacement.
- Wants scheduling, billing, insurance, payments, or patient portal features.
- Has no meaningful inquiry volume.
- Refuses to assign a follow-up owner.
- Expects guaranteed revenue.
- Needs enterprise or multi-location complexity immediately.
- Wants to use real patient data without privacy and hosting planning.
