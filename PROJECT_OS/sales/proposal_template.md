# Chiropractic Business OS Proposal Template

## 1. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines a simple Chiropractic Business OS setup to help `[Practice Name]` track patient inquiries, improve follow-up consistency, review weekly practice activity, and export inquiry data when needed.

The system is not an EHR, scheduling platform, billing system, insurance tool, payment processor, or patient portal. It is a focused inquiry and follow-up dashboard for practice visibility.

## 2. Current Practice Challenges

Based on discovery, the current workflow may include:

- Missed follow-ups.
- Patient inquiry visibility issues.
- Inconsistent inquiry tracking.
- Limited weekly reporting for the owner or office manager.
- Difficulty seeing which inquiries need attention.

## 3. Recommended Solution

The recommended solution is a Chiropractic Business OS setup that includes:

- Patient Inquiry Tracker: capture inquiry details, requested service, source, status, notes, and follow-up date.
- Follow-Up Dashboard: see overdue and due-today follow-ups.
- Weekly Practice Summary: review weekly inquiry activity and follow-up pressure.
- Estimated Treatment Value Dashboard: see estimated treatment value from inquiries not marked Lost.
- CSV Exports: download inquiry and follow-up data for review or handoff.
