# Pipeline Definitions

Use these stages to understand where each chiropractic prospect is in the sales process.

| Stage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Lead | Prospect has been identified but not yet qualified. |
| Qualified | Prospect appears to fit the target buyer profile and may have an inquiry/follow-up tracking pain. |
| Demo | Demo is scheduled or completed. |
| Proposal | Proposal, pricing, or paid pilot scope has been sent. |
| Decision | Prospect is deciding, reviewing timing, or discussing objections. |
| Won | Prospect agreed to move forward. |
| Lost | Prospect declined, is not a fit, or should no longer be pursued. |

## Stage Rules

- Do not move a prospect to `Qualified` without a reason.
- Do not move a prospect to `Proposal` until pricing or scope has actually been sent.
- Keep `Won` and `Lost` final unless a prospect reopens the conversation.
