# Client Health Score

Use this simple health framework to monitor chiropractic clients after handoff.

## Green

Client is healthy and engaged.

- Actively using the system.
- Weekly reviews completed.
- Follow-ups are being reviewed.
- Owner understands the dashboard.
- No major unresolved objections.

Recommended action:

- Continue monthly check-ins.
- Ask for light feedback.
- Look for testimonial or referral opportunities after clear value is visible.

## Yellow

Client is at risk but recoverable.

- Inconsistent usage.
- Missed follow-up reviews.
- Weekly summary not reviewed consistently.
- Front desk may be unclear on ownership.
- Client has questions or minor frustrations.

Recommended action:

- Schedule a focused check-in.
- Reconfirm the daily and weekly workflow.
- Identify one friction point.
- Help the client restart with a simple follow-up review habit.

## Red

Client is at high risk.

- Stopped using system.
- No engagement.
- Follow-up reviews are not happening.
- Owner is unresponsive.
- Client does not see value.

Recommended action:

- Request a direct retention call.
- Ask what stopped usage.
- Clarify whether the system still fits the workflow.
- Offer a narrow recovery plan or close the engagement cleanly.
