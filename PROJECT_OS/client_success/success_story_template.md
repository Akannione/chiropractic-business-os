# Success Story Template

Use this after a real client has used the Chiropractic Business OS long enough to show a credible operational win.

## 1. Practice Profile

```text
Practice name:
Location:
Practice size:
Primary contact:
Use period:
```

## 2. Challenge

Describe the problem before implementation.

Prompts:

- What made inquiry tracking difficult?
- Were follow-ups missed or hard to review?
- What did the owner or office manager struggle to see?

## 3. Previous Workflow

Describe how the practice handled inquiries before the Business OS.

Prompts:

- Spreadsheet, notes, inbox, EHR, scheduler, or memory?
- Who owned follow-up?
- What happened during busy weeks?

## 4. Solution

Describe what was implemented.

Include:

- Patient Inquiry Tracker
- Follow-Up Dashboard
- Weekly Practice Summary
- CSV Exports
- Staff Training

## 5. Results

Capture measurable or observable results.

Examples:

- Follow-ups became easier to review.
- Owner could see inquiry activity weekly.
- Staff had a clearer daily routine.
- Fewer inquiries were left without a next follow-up date.

Use only truthful results. Do not invent revenue outcomes.

## 7. Lessons Learned

Prompts:

- What training point mattered most?
