# Client Testimonial Request

Use this after the client has seen clear value. Do not ask too early.

## Testimonial Script

```text
Hi [Name],

I wanted to ask a quick favor. Since the Chiropractic Business OS has been helping your team track inquiries and follow-ups, would you be open to sharing a short testimonial?

It can be simple: what problem you were trying to solve, what changed after using the system, and what you would tell another practice owner considering it.

If helpful, I can draft a short version for your approval.
```

## Referral Script

```text
Hi [Name],

If you know another chiropractic practice owner who struggles with missed inquiries, front-desk follow-up, or weekly visibility, would you be comfortable introducing me?

I am not looking to pitch them aggressively. I would just like to show the same simple workflow and see whether it fits.
```

## Case Study Participation Script

```text
Hi [Name],

Would you be open to participating in a short case study about how your practice improved inquiry follow-up visibility?

The case study can stay focused on operations and outcomes. We can avoid sensitive patient details, and I will send the final version for approval before anything is shared.
```

## Ask Rules

- Ask only after value is visible.
- Keep the request short.
- Offer to draft the testimonial.
- Do not pressure the client.
- Get written approval before publishing names, quotes, or case study details.
