Purpose: track outreach progress toward the first chiropractic client.

This tracker lives inside `PROJECT_OS` and is not part of the application.

## Conversion Calculations

Use these calculations during weekly review:

```text
Contact attempt rate = Contacts Attempted / Prospects Identified
Response rate = Responses / Contacts Attempted
Discovery call rate = Discovery Calls / Responses
Demo rate = Demos / Discovery Calls
Proposal rate = Proposals / Demos
Client close rate = Clients / Proposals
Overall client conversion = Clients / Prospects Identified
```

If a denominator is zero, record the conversion as `0%` until activity exists.

## Operating Rule

Do not judge the system only by total prospects contacted. Review where the funnel slows down:

- Low response rate means messaging or prospect quality needs work.
- Low discovery-call rate means replies are not being converted into conversations.
- Low demo rate means discovery may not be creating enough fit.
- Low proposal rate means next steps are unclear.
- Low close rate means pricing, trust, urgency, or scope may need refinement.
