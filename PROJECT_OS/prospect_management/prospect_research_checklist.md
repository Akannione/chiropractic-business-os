## Website Review

Check:

- Clear phone number.
- Contact form.
- Request appointment or contact CTA.
- Services page.
- Provider or team page.
- Recent content or updates.
- Testimonials or reviews.

## Technology Signals

Look for:

- Online forms.
- Chat widget.
- Online booking request.
- Email capture.
- Review links.
- Active social links.
