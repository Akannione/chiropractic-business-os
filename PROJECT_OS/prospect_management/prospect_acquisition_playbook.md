# Prospect Acquisition Playbook

Goal: build, score, and prioritize 100 chiropractic practices before outreach.

This playbook focuses on Georgia chiropractic practices and supports outreach readiness without adding product features or changing the app.

## Objective

Create a qualified list of 100 chiropractic practices, identify the top 20 most likely buyers, and prepare consistent outreach using the existing discovery, proposal, and onboarding materials.

## Target Markets

Prioritize Georgia markets where chiropractic practices are likely to have meaningful inquiry flow and visible online activity:

- Atlanta metro
- Marietta
- Alpharetta
- Sandy Springs
- Decatur
- Roswell
- Johns Creek
- Lawrenceville
- Athens
- Savannah
- Augusta
- Macon
- Columbus

Start with metro and suburban practices where online reviews, websites, and inquiry channels are easier to evaluate.

## Georgia Strategy

Focus on small chiropractic practices that show signs of active patient acquisition:

- Google Business Profile with recent reviews.
- Website with a contact form or clear phone CTA.
- Services listed beyond generic chiropractic care.
- Evidence of owner-operated or small-team decision-making.
- Local SEO activity, blog posts, patient testimonials, or social activity.
- Multiple inquiry channels such as phone, website, Google, referral messaging, or insurance language.

Avoid starting with:

- Enterprise healthcare groups.
- Practices that appear inactive online.
- Practices clearly looking for EHR, billing, insurance, or scheduling tools.
- Practices with no obvious way to contact an owner or office manager.

## Daily Prospecting Targets

Use this daily target until 100 prospects are researched:

- Research 15 practices per day.
- Add 10 qualified prospects to the tracker per day.
- Score every added prospect.
- Flag top candidates immediately.
- Log red flags instead of forcing weak prospects into the list.

If time is limited, prioritize quality:

- 8 strong researched prospects are better than 20 weak entries.

## Weekly Prospecting Goals

Each week:

- Add 50-100 researched practices.
- Score all prospects.
- Identify at least 20 Tier 1 or strong Tier 2 prospects.
- Review red flags and remove low-priority practices.
- Prepare outreach order for the next week.
- Confirm discovery call materials and proposal package are ready.

## Prospect Research Workflow

For each practice:

1. Search by Georgia city and chiropractic practice.
2. Open the practice website.
3. Review Google Business Profile signals if available.
4. Record contact details in the prospect tracker.
5. Capture website quality and service diversity.
6. Estimate practice size.
7. Note technology signals.
8. Score the prospect.
9. Assign Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, or Low Priority.
10. Add follow-up notes and next action.

## Qualification Workflow

Use `qualification_scorecard.md`.

Score:

- Practice Size
- Monthly Inquiry Volume
- Current Tracking Method
- Follow-Up Process
- Reporting Maturity
- Openness to Technology

If data is unknown before outreach, use conservative scoring and mark the field as `Unknown` in notes. Re-score after discovery calls.

## Ranking Workflow

Rank prospects by:

1. Qualification score.
2. Tier assignment.
3. Strength of inquiry signals.
4. Evidence of follow-up need.
5. Website and review activity.
6. Decision-maker accessibility.
7. Low presence of red flags.

Top 20 should be mostly Tier 1 plus the strongest Tier 2 prospects.

## Outreach Readiness Rule

Do not launch outreach until:

- At least 100 prospects are researched.
- Every prospect has a score.
- Top 20 are identified.
- Discovery call playbook is reviewed.
- Demo website is ready.
- Proposal package is ready.
- Objection library is reviewed.
