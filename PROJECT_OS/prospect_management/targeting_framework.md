# Tiered Prospect Targeting Framework

Use this framework to prioritize chiropractic prospects before June 15 outreach. Pair it with `qualification_scorecard.md` and the prospect tracker workbook.

## Tier 1: Score 80-100

### Practice Characteristics

- Solo or small multi-provider chiropractic practice.
- Clear front-desk workflow but visible gaps in inquiry tracking.
- Uses spreadsheets, notes, inboxes, or memory for some inquiry follow-up.
- Owner or office manager cares about weekly visibility.
- Likely willing to review a simple demo and pilot.

### Expected Inquiry Volume

- 20+ patient inquiries per month, or enough phone/website/referral volume that follow-up consistency matters.

### Likelihood To Buy

- High. These practices are closest to the ideal customer profile and most likely to understand the value quickly.

### Outreach Priority

- Highest priority. Use direct phone, email, and follow-up sequences first.

### Recommended Follow-Up Intensity

- Initial email or call.
- Follow-up within 2 business days.
- Second follow-up within 5 business days.
- Offer demo video plus live walkthrough.
- Keep active until a clear yes, no, or timing objection is logged.

## Tier 2: Score 65-79

### Practice Characteristics

- Good fit but one buying signal is weaker.
- May have moderate inquiry volume, partial tracking process, or some technology hesitation.
- Could benefit from the dashboard but may need more trust-building.

### Expected Inquiry Volume

- 10-19 patient inquiries per month, or lower volume with clear follow-up inconsistency.

### Likelihood To Buy

- Medium-high. These practices may buy after a strong demo or clear ROI conversation.

### Outreach Priority

- Strong priority after Tier 1. Use structured outreach but avoid over-investing before a reply.

### Recommended Follow-Up Intensity

- Initial email.
- One phone call or LinkedIn/Facebook touch if contact info is available.
- Follow-up after 3-5 business days.
- Offer the demo video before pushing for a live walkthrough.

## Tier 3: Score 50-64

### Practice Characteristics

- Possible fit but missing important signals.
- Inquiry volume may be low or unclear.
- Current tracking method may already be decent.
- Owner may be skeptical, busy, or less open to new tools.

### Expected Inquiry Volume

- 5-9 patient inquiries per month, or unknown volume requiring discovery.

### Likelihood To Buy

- Medium-low. These prospects should not consume heavy outreach time unless discovery improves the score.

### Outreach Priority

- Lower priority. Use lighter-touch outreach and revisit after Tier 1 and Tier 2 prospects.

### Recommended Follow-Up Intensity

- One email or message.
- One follow-up if there is a relevant signal.
- Do not chase aggressively.
- Re-score only if they respond or new information appears.

## Below 50: Low Priority

Do not prioritize for June 15 outreach unless new information changes the score.

Common reasons:

- No clear inquiry volume.
- Wants EHR, billing, insurance, scheduling, or enterprise workflow features.
- Already has a mature CRM or reporting process.
- Not open to a simple pilot.
- No follow-up problem.
