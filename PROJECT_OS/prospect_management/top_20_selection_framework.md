# Top 20 Selection Framework

Use this framework to select the first 20 chiropractic practices most likely to become paying clients from a researched pool of 100.

## Core Criteria

Score and rank each prospect using:

- Qualification score
- Practice size
- Website quality
- Review activity
- Service diversity
- Marketing activity
- Technology adoption

## Automatic Tier 1 Indicators

A practice can be treated as a likely Tier 1 candidate when several of these are true:

- Qualification score is 80 or higher.
- Solo or small multi-provider practice.
- Strong Google review count and recent review activity.
- Website has clear phone CTA and contact form.
- Services page includes multiple chiropractic service lines.
- Marketing activity suggests active patient acquisition.
- No obvious EHR, billing, scheduling, or enterprise-only buying requirement.
- Owner or office manager appears reachable.

## Automatic Red Flags

Move the practice down or deprioritize when any of these appear:

- Wants or appears to need an EHR replacement.
- Needs billing, insurance, scheduling, payment, or patient portal functionality.
- Enterprise healthcare group or multi-location system with complex procurement.
- No clear inquiry volume.
- No website or meaningful online presence.
- No obvious contact path.
- Existing CRM or reporting process appears mature.
- Strong privacy or compliance requirement before discovery.

## Tie-Breaker Criteria

If two practices score similarly, prioritize the practice with:

1. More recent review activity.
2. Better website contact flow.
3. More service diversity.
4. Clearer small-practice decision-maker access.
5. Stronger signs of marketing spend or lead flow.
6. More visible follow-up risk.
7. Higher likelihood of taking a simple demo seriously.

## Ranking Method

1. Score all 100 prospects.
2. Remove obvious red flags.
3. Sort by qualification score.
4. Assign Tier 1, Tier 2, or Tier 3.
5. Apply tie-breakers.
6. Select top 20.
7. Review the list manually before outreach.
