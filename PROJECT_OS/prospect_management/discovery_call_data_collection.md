# Discovery Call Data Collection

Use this during discovery calls to improve a prospect's qualification score after live conversation.

## Inquiry Volume

Questions:

- How many new patient inquiries do you receive in a typical month?
- Which channels create the most inquiries: phone, Google, website, referrals, insurance, or walk-ins?
- Does inquiry volume change by season?
- How many inquiries do not book immediately?

Score impact:

- Higher inquiry volume increases fit.
- Unknown volume should be clarified before sending a proposal.

## Tracking Method

Questions:

- Where do you track new patient inquiries today?
- Do you use a spreadsheet, notes, EHR, scheduler, CRM, or inbox?
- What happens when someone calls but does not book right away?
- Can the owner quickly see all open inquiries?

Score impact:

- Spreadsheet, notes, inboxes, or memory are strong fit signals.
- Mature CRM or reliable EHR workflow may reduce fit.

## Follow-Up Process

Questions:

- Who owns follow-up after an inquiry?
- How do you know which follow-ups are due today?
- How do you know which follow-ups are overdue?
- What usually causes follow-ups to slip?

Score impact:

- Unclear ownership or overdue follow-ups increase fit.
- Strong existing ownership may reduce urgency.

## Reporting Process

Questions:

- Do you review inquiry activity weekly?
- Can you see how many inquiries became active patients?
- Can you see which inquiry source performs best?
- Do you review estimated treatment value from open inquiries?

Score impact:

- Limited weekly reporting increases fit.
- Strong existing reporting may reduce fit.

## Decision-Maker Identification

Questions:

- Who decides whether to move forward with a simple dashboard like this?
- Who would use it day to day?
- Who should attend training?
- Is there anyone else who needs to approve the setup?

Score impact:

- Clear owner or office manager decision-maker increases fit.
- Unclear decision process can slow or reduce priority.

## Technology Openness

Questions:

- Are you open to a simple pilot if it does not replace your existing systems?
- What tools has your practice adopted successfully before?
- What made previous tools hard to use?
- Would a four-day setup and training process feel reasonable?

Score impact:

- Openness to simple workflow tools increases fit.
- Strong resistance to new processes reduces priority.
